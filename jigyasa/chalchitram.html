Chalchitram

Short Movie Making

“You shouldn’t dream your film, you should make it!” - Steven Spielberg . Movies allow us to enter other minds, not simply in the sense of identifying with the characters, but by seeing the world as another person sees it. Do you often find yourself immersed in this world? Have you ever wondered what it would feel like if this was not only your passion but also your profession? Jigyasa aims to provide you with the first stepping stone.

How to participate?

Register Here

Rules

Team Size: At most 4 in the core team (No particular limit for actors in the film)

Rounds: Entries to be submitted online. Choose any of theme provided.

Judging Criteria: Editing, Cinematography, Script (originality), Acting, Sound effects, overall impact, Relevance to theme.

Elimination

Time Limit: 2-5 minutes (Including credits)

Themes : The Cultural Heritage of India, Pilgrim sites in India, Holy places in India

Deadline for submission: 24th August

General Rules

The movie should be in Hindi or English or both. If you choose to make a film in Hindi, it must contain English subtitles. * No other languages will be allowed.

You may use any video camera you deem suitable for shooting the movie. However, we strongly discourage low-resolution cameras, including webcams and mobile-phone cameras.

The content of the movie should be appropriate for public screening and thus have no vulgarity/Obscenity. Sound tracks (no max time limit) and stock footage (time limit : max of 1 min) can be borrowed, but with due credits.

The decision of the judges shall be final and binding.
