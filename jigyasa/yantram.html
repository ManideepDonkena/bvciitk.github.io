Yantram

Machine Learning Competition

AI is at the cusp of today’s technology frontier. Bhagavad Gita is the most ancient science dwelling on topics which have intrigued human minds of all ages. Here is an opportunity to learn and use AI to mine the depths of Bhagavad Gita. This year in Jigyasa-2k19, we are organizing a competition on AI and NLP aimed at building an information retrieval system to get answers from Bhagavad Gita for the queries asked by users. To help you, there will be a bootcamp facilitated by IIT Kanpur and IIT Kharagpur professors. Your systems will pass through two stages of evaluation. The best ones will be demonstrated at Jigyasa 2019 at Auditorium, IIT Kanpur, and handsomely rewarded, of course. There is no prerequisite. Kick start your AI expedition this Jigyasa.
