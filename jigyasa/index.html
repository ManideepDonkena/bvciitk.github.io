About JIGYASA

Jigyasa is here at IITK to make you think, to drench you with questions regarding the self. It’s time to confront your Inner Self and realise your true potential. We are organising events for IIT Kanpur students such as dance, drama, music ,treasure hunt, pencil painting, quizzes, debates etc which would be designed to inquire about one’s innerself. Who am I? What is real success? Am I eternal? And much more….. Looks fascinating? Do like the page to stay tuned and to win exciting prizes such as Alexa, Kindle, Fastrack watches and much more. I assure you all that this journey is going to be pristine and thrilling You will gain indelible experiences

Yantram

Machine Learning-Here is an opportunity to learn and use AI to mine the depths of Bhagavad Gita. This year in Jigyasa-2k19, we are organizing a competition on AI and NLP aimed at building an information retrieval system to get answers from Bhagavad Gita for the queries asked by users. To help you, there will be a bootcamp facilitated by IIT Kanpur and IIT Kharagpur professors. Your systems will pass through two stages of evaluation. The best ones will be demonstrated at Jigyasa 2019 at Auditorium, IIT Kanpur, and handsomely rewarded, of course. There is no prerequisite.

Swaranjali

Sloka Recitation- Bhagavad Gita was spoken directly by the supreme counsellor and psychologist none other than Lord Sri Krishna. The therapeutic effects of traditional shloka recitation are not unknown to our ancestors. If one recites and reflects on the Gita regularly and attentively, he will gain access to newer and higher truths of life through which he can lead a lively yet thoughtful and balanced life. If a person just hears Bhagavad Gita at the fag end of his life, he gets freed from all lamentations and anxieties arisen from debacles committed in his life. Then what to speak of a person who regularly chants it.
